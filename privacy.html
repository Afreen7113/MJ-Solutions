Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how MJ Smart Solution collects, uses, and protects information in connection with our AI platform and related services.

This example text is for demonstration purposes only and does not constitute legal advice.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Account Information – such as your name, company name, job title, and contact details.
  • Usage Data – such as log files, IP addresses, device identifiers, browser type, and interactions with our services.
  • Customer Data – data that you upload or connect to our platform as part of your use of the services.
  • Support Communications – information you provide when you contact us for help or feedback.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve the services;
  • Configure and maintain user accounts and workspaces;
  • Monitor performance, security, and usage trends;
  • Respond to inquiries and provide customer support;
  • Send service‑related communications, updates, and security alerts;
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.

3. Legal Bases for Processing (EEA/UK only)

Where required by law, we process personal data on the following legal bases:

  • To perform our contract with you or your organization;
  • With your consent, where applicable;
  • For our legitimate interests, such as improving and securing our services;
  • To comply with legal obligations.

4. How We Share Information

We may share information with:

  • Service providers that assist us with hosting, analytics, support, and security;
  • Partners where you have authorized an integration or third‑party service;
  • Authorities when required by law, legal process, or to protect rights and safety;
  • Other parties in connection with a corporate transaction, such as a merger or acquisition.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as necessary to provide the services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Retention periods may vary based on the type of data and the context in which it was collected.

6. Security

We implement technical and organizational measures designed to protect personal data against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. International Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. Where required, we implement appropriate safeguards, such as standard contractual clauses, to protect data transferred internationally.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to certain processing, as well as data portability. To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details below.

9. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal data, we will take steps to delete such information.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you by posting an updated version on our website or by other appropriate means. Your continued use of the services after the effective date of any changes indicates acceptance of the updated Policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at ops@mj-smartsolutions.ai.
